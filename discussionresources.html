Discussion Resources

If you are interested in joining St Andrews Fashion Collective we have included some resources to tell you more about how to pursue a career in fashion. We found the Alexa Chung documentary series, which was commissioned by British Vogue, very informative as an insight into the many aspects that make up the fashion industry. Alexa Chung includes interviews with individuals working in fashion journalism, design, buisness, law and photography.
